01 — Problem

Why farmers lose money

Kazakhstan is one of the largest pastoral countries in the world. 70% of agricultural land is pasture. Yet farmers still use 100-year-old methods.

01

400,000 heads lost annually

5% of livestock is lost every year — theft, predators, straying. Billions of tenge in damages nationwide.

02

Fencing: $1,500–2,500/hectare

Physical fencing is extremely expensive. For rotational grazing, stationary fences are useless.

03

Shepherd: $300–600/month

Human factor, fatigue, illness. A shepherd cannot watch 24/7 and lacks full situational awareness.

04

No pasture data

40% of land is used irrationally. No tools to show where herds grazed and what has recovered.

02 — Solution

One tag — full control

SteppeAI leverages herd behavior: all animals follow the leader. A single IoT tag on the leader is enough.

1

Tag on the leader

IoT device is attached to the herd leader (bull, ram, stallion). Battery life 6+ months.

2

Virtual fence

Farmer draws the pasture zone on a map. The system creates a "smart invisible fence" — AI-Fence.

3

AI monitoring 24/7

Coordinates every 30 seconds. AI analyzes movement, speed, stops, anomalies and escape risk.

4

Instant alerts

When crossing boundaries → push notification via Telegram/SMS. Farmer reacts in minutes, not hours.

up to $2,000/year

Savings for a farmer with 100 heads by reducing shepherd costs and preventing losses
